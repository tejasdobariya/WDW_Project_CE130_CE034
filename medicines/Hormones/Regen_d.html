INTRODUCTION

Description
Regen-D 150 Gel is used in the treatment of diabetic foot ulcers that occur as a complication of diabetes. It helps in the quick healing of the sores (ulcers) of the leg, ankle, or foot of diabetic patients. Regen-D 150 Gel accelerates healing and formation of healthy skin, thus providing relief in the skin infection. It is meant for external use only. You should use it in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor.

USES OF Regen-D 150 Gel
Diabetic foot ulcers

Key Benefits
 Diabetic foot ulcers are painful open sores or wounds that usually occur in diabetic patients on the leg, ankle, bottom, or top portion of the foot or on the tips of the toes. If severely infected, hospitalization may also be needed in such patients. Regen-D 150 Gel is used for the treatment of diabetic foot ulcers.
